🎋 Welcome to Lao Wang Tea Shop! 🎋

欢迎光临 · 老王茶叶店 · 正宗福建茶

Hello and welcome!! My name is Wang Mingzhi, I am third generation of Lao Wang Tea Shop. My grandfather start this shop in 1962 in Fuzhou, Fujian Province. We are specialize in authentic Fujian tea — Tieguanyin oolong, Wuyi rock tea, white tea from Fuding, and aged Pu-erh from Yunnan. All our tea come directly from farmers we know personally. No middleman!

We ship international since 2008. Many customer from America, Europe, Australia already enjoy our tea. We pack carefully with vacuum seal to keep freshness. If you have any question please contact us by email or WeChat anytime!!
