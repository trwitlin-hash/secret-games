☕ VISIT US — FREE TEA TASTING!

Welcome to our shop — come try before you buy!

When you visit Lao Wang Tea Shop in person, we always offer complimentary tea tasting. Our tea master will prepare fresh gongfu tea and explain the differences between varieties. No pressure to buy — we just love sharing our passion for good tea!

On some Saturdays we hold special tea appreciation events (茶艺表演) — follow our WeChat page for schedule. These events are free to attend and very educational. Families welcome!

We are easy to find — look for the red lanterns outside on Zhongshan Road. You will smell the tea before you see the shop! 🍵
